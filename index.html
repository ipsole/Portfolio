Workflow Stages

1. Inquiry & Scope

You reach out with your vision. We quickly review your needs and align on initial scope and expectations.

2. Discovery Meeting

A dedicated meeting to dive deep into your brand, target audience, and content style. We present a custom workflow solution.

3. Proposal & Deal

We confirm the package, timeline, and deliverables. Upon mutual agreement, the deal is confirmed and contract is prepared.

4. Secure Payment

A small advance (20% for new clients) settles the initial agreement and secures your slot in our production schedule.

5. Client Onboarding

You are onboarded to our system. We receive your raw footage and brand assets, ensuring everything is ready for production.

6. Production Kick-off

Our editors execute the vision, focusing on high-impact edits, motion, and sound design exactly as specified in the brief.

7. Review & Finalize

You review the first draft and provide notes. We refine and polish the edit until it perfectly matches your vision.

8. Delivery & Success

Final payment is settled, and you receive the high-quality, high-impact video ready for publishing.
